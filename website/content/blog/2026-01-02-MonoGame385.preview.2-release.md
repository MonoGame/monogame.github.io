## 3.8.5-preview.2 Release Announcement

The MonoGame Foundation is pleased to announce Preview 2 of the 3.8.5 Release!
[Grab it here](https://www.nuget.org/packages?q=MonoGame) or from within your favourite IDE.

Highlights is this release are the new StartKit templates, with VK/DX12 targets included.
Also of note:

* It includes multiple platforms, if you do not have the iOS / Android workloads, then just remove them from the solution to test.
* No additional requirements from the other platforms.
* Vulkan and DX12 are previews and there may be issues.  Please log them accordingly here.

Please help test this latest version!
